To start, guess any 5-letter word.

Press the letter keys on your keyboard, or the on-screen keys to type your guess.

When you are ready, press the "GUESS" button or the "Enter" key on your keyboard.

After you make a guess, the letters will change color to give you clues!

    Gray means the letter is not in the word at all.
    Pink means the letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
    Cyan means the letter is correct AND in the right position!

Next, you can make another guess! Use the clues from your guesses to figure out the secret word.

You have 6 guesses, if you run out of guesses, the game is over!

Reload the page to try again.

Good luck!
